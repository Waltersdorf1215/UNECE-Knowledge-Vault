# GRVA Timeline

Chronological history of GRVA (Working Party on Automated/Autonomous and Connected Vehicles) sessions and key decisions.

---

## GRVA Background

**Fact:** GRVA was established in 2018/2019 as successor to GRRF, taking over responsibility for automated and connected vehicle regulations under WP.29.
