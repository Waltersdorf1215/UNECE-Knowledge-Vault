## R157 Original Scope

**Fact (from GTR §82):** "Currently, there is only one specific ADS application for which a UN ADS regulation has been developed (ALKS/R157)."

**Known scope:**
- SAE Level 3
- Motorway only
- Original speed: ≤60 km/h

## Relationship to ADS UN GTR

**Fact (GTR §77/§82):** The ADS UN GTR is designed to cover all ADS applications *except* ALKS (which remains under R157). The GTR provides the general framework; R157 remains the specific instrument for ALKS.

*Interpretation:* An ALKS vehicle under R157 would likely be an **ADSF-1** feature in GTR terms (has human fallback requirement).
