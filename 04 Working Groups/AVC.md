# AVC — Automated Vehicle Categorisation

## Summary

**Fact (ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139, §18):** AVC (Automated Vehicle Categorisation) is an informal working group under [[GRVA]] that provided reports to the [[ADS IWG]] during the development of the [[ADS UN GTR]].

> "The IWG also received reports on the work of other informal groups, including Automated Vehicle Categorisation (AVC)..."

---

## Scope

AVC focuses on developing a taxonomy and categorisation framework for automated vehicles, which feeds into regulatory definitions used in the [[ADS UN GTR]] (e.g., the [[ADSF-1 and ADSF-2]] distinction).
