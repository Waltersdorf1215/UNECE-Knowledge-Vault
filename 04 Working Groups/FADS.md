# FADS — Regulation Fitness for Automated Driving Systems

## Summary

**Fact (ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139, §18 and §45):** FADS (Regulation Fitness for Automated Driving Systems) is a Task Force under [[GRVA]] that provided reports to the [[ADS IWG]] during development of the [[ADS UN GTR]].

> "The IWG also received reports on the work of other informal groups, including... Regulation Fitness for Automated Driving Systems (FADS)..."

**Fact (§45):** "There are several GRVA subgroups active in the field of vehicle automation (EDR/DSSAD, TF on AVC, TF on FADS, Cybersecurity/Over-the-Air software updates)."

---

## Scope

FADS assesses the fitness of existing UN Regulations (such as [[R79]], [[R157]], [[R171]], [[R155]], [[R156]]) to accommodate ADS. Identifies regulatory gaps and conflicts that need to be resolved as ADS is deployed under the new regulatory framework.

*Interpretation:* FADS is the "gap analysis" group — it maps existing regulations against ADS requirements and flags where amendments or new provisions are needed.
