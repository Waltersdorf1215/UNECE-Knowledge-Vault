# OEDR — Object and Event Detection and Response

## Definition

**Fact (ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139, §41(d)):**
> "The automated vehicles shall be able to detect and respond to objects/events that may be reasonably expected in the ODD."

---

## Role in ADS Requirements

**Fact (§4.1.2.4):** "The ADS shall detect and respond to objects and events relevant to its performance of the DDT."

OEDR is a core component of the DDT's **sensing and perception** and **control** functions:
- Sensing and perception includes "monitoring the driving environment via object and event detection, recognition, and classification" (§2.3.2)
- Control includes "object and event response execution" (§2.3.4)

---

## OEDR in Validation

**Fact (Annex 5, §2.1.3):** The OEDR Analysis is the third step in the [[Behavioural Competency]] identification framework:
> Once objects and their reasonably expected behaviours have been identified, the OEDR analysis maps the appropriate ADS response — expressed as a behavioural competency.
