# Artificial Intelligence

## Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a cross-cutting technology area relevant to automated driving, software-defined vehicles, cybersecurity, and vehicle data governance. The EME WG SDV-AVS-AI meeting gap analysis identifies AI as a High priority missing concept and as part of the working group's scope.

## Background

**Fact (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, Article 3(1), official):** The EU AI Act defines an AI system as a machine-based system designed to operate with varying levels of autonomy and capable of generating outputs such as predictions, content, recommendations, or decisions.

Within the UNECE Knowledge OS, AI should be tracked carefully because it may appear in multiple contexts: automated driving system behavior, driver assistance, software-defined vehicle architectures, cybersecurity, data governance, and future GRVA discussions.

## Purpose

This note provides a canonical concept page for AI as it appears in UNECE-adjacent regulatory and industry discussions. It should separate official legal definitions from meeting discussion, industry terminology, and interpretation.
