# GRVA 24th Session

## Summary

The 24th session of GRVA (Working Party on Automated/Autonomous and Connected Vehicles), held in January 2026.

**Fact:** This session recommended the draft UN GTR on Automated Driving Systems to WP.29 for consideration at the [[WP29 199th Session]] (June 2026).

> "It was recommended by the Working Party on Automated/Autonomous and Connected Vehicles (GRVA) at its twenty-fourth session."
— **Source:** ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139, page 1

The basis document submitted to WP.29 was **ECE/TRANS/WP.29/GRVA/2026/2**, together with informal document **WP.29-198-07**.

---

## Key Confirmed Outcome

**Fact:** GRVA 24th session:
- **Recommended** the draft [[ADS UN GTR]] (ECE/TRANS/WP.29/GRVA/2026/2) for WP.29 consideration
- Submitted the proposal as ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139 to [[WP29 199th Session]]

The GTR text contains provisions in [square brackets] to be reconfirmed by GRVA at its **[[GRVA 25th Session]]**.

## Notes on Text in Square Brackets

**Fact:** Some GTR provisions are in [square brackets] and require reconfirmation by GRVA at its 25th session. This is noted on the cover page of ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139.

*Interpretation:* This means the GTR as submitted to WP.29 199th is not fully finalized — certain provisions remain under deliberation. The WP.29 vote may be conditional or provisional on GRVA 25th resolution.

## Additional Confirmed Outcome — R171 Phase 3

**Fact (ADAS-45-07, p.2, E-Mobility Europe, 2026-03-03):** "Major progress was achieved with DCAS phase 3 developments and **successful GRVA 24th meeting!**"

This independently confirms that the GRVA 24th session endorsed the **02 series amendments to R171** (DCAS Phase 3). Phase 3 covers: Withholding HOR & SIM on Highway and Highway-like roads, SIM outside Highway, and natural driving requirements.

Source: `12 Attachments/UNECE/ADAS-45-07-EME-Inputs-Next-Steps.pdf` — submitted by [[E-Mobility Europe]] (EME) at ADAS 45th session (2026-03-03).
