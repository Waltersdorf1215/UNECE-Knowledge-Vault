# ADR-005 Evidence First

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Regulatory knowledge must remain trustworthy. Unsupported claims can mislead future research and damage the reliability of the Vault.

## Decision

The Knowledge OS follows an Evidence First policy. Official and traceable evidence takes priority over memory, interpretation, or convenience.

## Rationale

Evidence-first knowledge supports verification, review, and long-term maintenance. It also makes uncertainty visible.

## Consequences

- Claims without evidence should be marked for verification.
- Official UNECE sources and official organization sources are preferred.
- Review evaluates evidence coverage.
- AI prior knowledge is never treated as official evidence.
