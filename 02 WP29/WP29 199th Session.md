# WP.29 199th Session

## Summary

The 199th session of the World Forum for Harmonization of Vehicle Regulations (WP.29), held in **Geneva, 23–26 June 2026**.

**Fact:** The [[ADS UN GTR]] (ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139) was submitted to this session for consideration and vote by **AC.3** (Executive Committee of the 1998 Agreement) under **Agenda Item 14.1.1**.

> "Consideration and vote by AC.3 of draft UN GTRs and/or draft amendments to established UN GTRs, if any: Proposal for a new UN GTR, if any: Proposal for a draft UN GTR on Automated Driving System"
— **Source:** ECE/TRANS/WP.29/2026/139, cover page

## AC.3 Vote Context

**Fact:** The ADS UN GTR requires an AC.3 vote. AC.3 is the Executive Committee of the 1998 Agreement, whose contracting parties include:
- United States (NHTSA)
- European Union (EC)
- Japan
- China
- Canada
- United Kingdom
- And others

The IWG on ADS was sponsored by **Canada, China, EC, Japan, UK, USA** — all key AC.3 parties, suggesting broad alignment on the text.
