# E-Mobility Europe (EME)

## Summary

**Fact (ADAS-45-07, p.6; emobilityeurope.org):** E-Mobility Europe (abbreviated **EME** in UNECE documents) is a European trade association representing the e-mobility ecosystem. It actively participates in UNECE WP.29 GRVA working groups, particularly the **ADAS Task Force**, where it has submitted technical proposals on DCAS regulation development through Phases 3 and 4.

## R171 Development Phases (as described by EME)

**Fact (ADAS-45-07, p.3):** EME's document describes the R171 amendment series as follows:

| Phase | Amendment Series | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | 00 series (original) | Lat & Long Assistance, DMS, EOR & HOR, Driver Confirmed Manoeuvre |
| Phase 2 | 01 series | Withholding HOR on Highway, SIM on Highway |
| Phase 3 | 02 series | Withholding HOR & SIM on Highway and Highway-like roads, SIM outside Highway, natural driving requirements (low speed, etc.) |
| Phase 4 (proposed) | 03 series | Withholding HOR & SIM on all road types; Legacy requirement restructuring; Non-rule-based algorithm systems |

New acronyms from this document (not yet in vault):
- **wHOR** = Withholding HOR — system capability to delay the Hands-On Request under safe conditions
- **SIM** = System-Initiated Manoeuvre — a lane change or manoeuvre initiated by the DCAS system without explicit driver command

### Phase 4 Outlook

**Fact (ADAS-45-07, p.3–4):** EME recommends TF-ADAS:
- Remains active and works toward Phase 4 (03 series) of R171
- Considers a 3–6 month break from active meetings, with SDG / Interested Parties preparing proposals
- Holds a plenary TF-ADAS meeting before September 2026 GRVA session
- Resumes active drafting of 03 series from September 2026 onward
